1)Hygenie
Unlike the big brands we do not collect our supplies from thousands of farmers where hygiene is not monitored, rather we have our own farms and cattle. The freshness and hygiene is looked after by highly trained supervisors.

2)Supervision
We keep a stringent check on the food quality and processes at the farm. Samples of our vegetables, milk are regularly checked and passed by FSSAI accredited labs for any trace of chemicals, pesticides or toxins.

3)Sanitation
We ensure our kitchen shelfs, sinks and cooking stations are washed multiple times during the day. The utensils are competely washed and sterlized before use.

4)Transportation Preparation
All the food items are steamed and it is ensured that the food is not spoiled during the transportation. Then the food is packed in vacum containers and kept in air tight metalic boxes ready for shipping.

5)Shipping
The metallic boxes are loaded into the cargo rocket and the food is shipped to mars for your consumption.

6)Retail
The retailing shops recieve the metallic boxes and deliver it to the respective order addresses.
